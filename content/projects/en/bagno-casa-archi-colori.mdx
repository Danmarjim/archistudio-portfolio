This bathroom is a hymn to balance: an oasis where time slows down to let you breathe, yet one that knows how to give you the right spark to take on the day.

Everything invites touch and relaxation: the reeded surfaces that animate the walls like gentle waves, the curved furniture and the arched wardrobe that seem to shelter your beauty rituals, the round lamps and circular mirror — small suns that light up the morning.

Powder blue dresses the space with its dusty, calm elegance, creating a cocoon of serenity. But the heart of the project beats in the orange details — vibrant, bold, vitamin-charged. It is the perfect colour to recharge under the shower spray before diving into the world.

The bathroom is no longer merely a functional space, but a recharge for the soul.
