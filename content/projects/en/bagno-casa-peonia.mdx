The ground-floor bathroom is a small jewel of chromatic coherence. The wallpaper lining the upper section echoes the violet and yellow tones of the kitchen-living area, while the ikat motifs deliberately recall the weave of the Sardinian rug displayed in the dining room.

The vanity unit was designed to measure: it includes a cupboard to conceal cleaning appliances and shelves to display the perfume bottle collection. Everything is in the same ivory as the walls of the house, for a soft and coherent aesthetic.

The warm yellow of the bathroom converses with the plum colour of the adjacent kitchen, creating an emotional continuum between the two spaces.
