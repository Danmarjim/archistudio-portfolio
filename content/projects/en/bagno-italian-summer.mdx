The master bathroom on the upper floor was renovated in an "Italian summer" style. The striped concept beloved by the client is resolved through the juxtaposition of elegant vertical-format tiles in glossy ceramic with irregular edges.

Semicircular shapes are the common thread throughout: they reappear in the mirrors, handles and accessories. The bathtub was replaced with a walk-in shower in chromatic continuity with the floor.

Step inside and blue envelops you — together with the stripes, it makes you feel by the sea all year round. A place to unwind and take care of yourself.
