The Loving Colors flat was published in **Cose di Casa no. 10, October 2022**, in the section dedicated to homes between 50 and 100 sqm.

A classically laid-out flat, now dated, takes on an open and contemporary layout that makes the interiors more liveable and better able to welcome natural light. On the decorative side, it is the bold colours inspired by the Hispanic tradition that define the character and personality of the home: from the terracotta shade of the large wardrobe-wall in the open living room, to the petrol blue of the kitchen, to the bright green of the bedroom.

The optimisation of the floor area and the living comfort achieved are largely due to the custom-made furniture designed by the architect, as part of a comprehensive interior design project full of clever, space-saving solutions. The standout one: a complete home office inside the shelving unit behind the sofa.
